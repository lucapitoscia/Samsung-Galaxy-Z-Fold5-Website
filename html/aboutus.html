Discover our story, values, and the journey that has shaped our legacy in technology and innovation.

Key Milestones

  • 1969 - Launch of Samsung Electronics, marking our official entry into the electronics industry.
  • 1988 - Introduction of our first mobile phone, laying the groundwork for mobile communication innovation.
  • 2009 - Samsung Galaxy S series debut, establishing our leadership in the smartphone market.
  • 2020 - Unveiling of the Galaxy Z Fold series, redefining the smartphone experience with foldable technology.

Innovation at Samsung

Our relentless pursuit of breakthrough technologies has led to pioneering advancements in foldable phones, quantum dot displays, and artificial intelligence, setting new benchmarks for the industry.

Commitment to Sustainability

Understanding our responsibility towards the planet, we are committed to sustainable manufacturing, reducing carbon emissions, and developing eco-friendly products.

Engagement and Community

We believe in giving back to the community through various initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and environmental conservation, making a positive impact worldwide.
